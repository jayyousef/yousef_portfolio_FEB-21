About Me!

Hi!

I'm a full stack developer who loves to work in both front-end and back-end! I'm based in St. Louis MO, and specialize in designing functional web-applications and creating attractive, high-end UX.

I consider myself to be extremely fortunate, because my ‘job’, such as it is, generally entails doing what I love. I call myself a designer as it’s a nice umbrella term for the web design / content creation / branding work that I do, although I spend a great deal of time writing, filming, and animating to help tell brand stories.

Lots of nice people have been kind enough to teach me their skills, which has made me an extremely well rounded and capable designer, always willing to learn new techniques and emergenty technology to create unique, meaningful content.

Outside of design and all of its connected parts, I love playing my grand piano, my Taylor guitar, my fender bass, and playing with my wife and two boys, Wilson and Milo.
